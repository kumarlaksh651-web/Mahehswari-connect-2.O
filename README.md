# Maheshwari Connect

Maheshwari Connect is a dedicated community platform designed to bridge the gap between members of the Maheshwari community worldwide. The application provides a centralized, digital space to foster meaningful relationships, preserve cultural ties, and empower community members to support one another through integrated social features.

## 📌 Purpose
The core mission of Maheshwari Connect is to build unity and eliminate geographical barriers within the community. By leveraging modern web technologies, the app ensures that every member feels valued, supported, and deeply connected to their roots and peers.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3
* **Backend:** JavaScript (Node.js)
* **Development & Debugging:** Python (Automation libraries), Replit IDE

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Global Community Directory:** Seamlessly find and connect with other community members.
* **Three-Tier Donation System:** A streamlined module allowing members to contribute financially or through resources based on three distinct paths:
  1. *Emergency Relief:* Immediate support for urgent medical or crisis situations.
  2. *Educational Support:* Sponsorships and funds for students pursuing higher education.
  3. *Community Welfare:* General contributions for cultural events and local community development.
